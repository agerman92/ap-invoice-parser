Pipeline Overview

What happens after you upload.

1Storage
PDF is stored in the ap-invoices Supabase bucket under manual-tests/.
2Invoice Record
A row is created in ap_invoices with status uploaded and review status unreviewed.
3Parse Queue
enqueue-invoice creates a durable parse job per file. Workers process them in parallel.
4AP Review
Once extracted, each invoice appears in the Review Queue for AP validation and approval.
