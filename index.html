Pipeline Summary

Current flow for upload and processing.

Storage
PDF uploads land in the ap-invoices bucket.
Invoice Record
A new row is inserted into ap_invoices with status uploaded.
Queue
The system calls enqueue-invoice and creates a durable parse job.
Worker
The worker processes the invoice asynchronously and routes it into vendor-specific parsing when available.
